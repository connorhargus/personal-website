What is this?
I once taught AP Calculus AB and Precalculus. In the process, I made a bunch of resources which I am sharing below in the hopes they may help other first-time math teachers. The PDF files are available for download below. The PDFs and all plots and figures therein are generated using TeX files which are also available below. Feel free to take them and adapt them to your needs without attribution.
